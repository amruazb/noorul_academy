ALL COURSES

Our Five Faculties

📖
Hiflul Quran

The complete memorisation of the Holy Quran. Students progress Juz by Juz under supervision of a qualified Hafidha. Regular revision sessions ensure long-term retention. Completion is celebrated with formal certificates and Dua ceremonies. Available for girls and boys under 15.

🎙️
Tajweed

A systematic study of the rules of Tajweed — Makharij al-Huroof, Sifaat, Madd rules, and more. Students learn to recite the Quran as it was revealed, following the rules passed down through chains of transmission from the Prophet ﷺ.

🌙
Nadhra Course

Designed for absolute beginners and young children learning to read Arabic. Students progress from the alphabet (Qaida Nooraniya) to reading full surahs and eventually the entire Quran fluently from the mushaf.

💎
Tarbiyyat Course

Islamic character education for young Muslims. Covers essential duas, prayers, adab with parents and teachers, Islamic values, Seerah of the Prophet ﷺ, and building a love for Allah and His Messenger. Integrated into all programmes.

🔄
Daura (Revision Course)

An intensive programme for students who have completed Hifz to revise and strengthen their memorisation. Structured Daura sessions cover the full 30 Juz systematically. Suitable for Huffaz seeking to maintain and perfect their Hifz.
